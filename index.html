About Us

Our Mission

When Founder & President Mike Yadlosky looked around campus, he kept hitting the same wall: there was no place for business students to practice thinking like consultants.

No structured experience. No client work. No framework for turning classroom learning into something that actually helps people. So he built it. Hatter Consulting exists to provide undergraduate students with hands-on consulting experience while delivering meaningful strategic value to local businesses, nonprofits, and entrepreneurs in the DeLand and Central Florida community.

Membership is open to all majors. Whether you're interested in strategy, finance, marketing, or operations — if you want real experience, this is where you build it.

Strategy

Frame ambiguous problems, build structured analyses, and deliver clear, actionable recommendations to real clients.

Collaboration

Work alongside driven peers, faculty mentors, and community partners on cross-functional project teams.

Impact

Deliver solutions that genuinely move the needle for clients in DeLand and beyond — not just slide decks.

Programs

What We Do

Four pillars. One purpose: real consulting experience for every Stetson student.

Client Work

Client Engagements

Teams of 3–5 students take on real projects for local businesses and nonprofits — delivering strategy memos, market research, and operational recommendations.

Workshops

Skills Workshops

Monthly sessions on consulting frameworks, case analysis, financial modeling, and presentation skills — training students to think and communicate like professionals.

Speaker Series

Speaker Series

Each semester, professionals from consulting, entrepreneurship, and finance share real career insights and help connect our members to the industry.

Competitions

Case Competitions

Structured preparation for regional and national case competitions — building résumés and putting Stetson students on the map alongside top universities.
