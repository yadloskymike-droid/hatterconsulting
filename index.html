About Us

Our Mission

Hatter Consulting was founded to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world business challenges.

We partner with local businesses and nonprofits to deliver data-driven solutions while developing the next generation of business leaders. Our members gain hands-on experience tackling complex problems across strategy, operations, marketing, and finance — all while making a tangible impact on the DeLand community.

Whether you're a first-year student eager to apply what you've learned, or a senior looking to build a portfolio that stands out, Hatter Consulting gives you the platform to grow.

Strategy

Frame ambiguous problems, build structured analyses, and deliver clear, actionable recommendations to real clients.

Collaboration

Work alongside driven peers, faculty mentors, and community partners on cross-functional project teams.

Impact

Deliver solutions that genuinely move the needle for clients in DeLand and beyond — not just slide decks.
