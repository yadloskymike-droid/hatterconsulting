Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Hatter Consulting ("we," "us," or "our") is a registered undergraduate student organization at Stetson University in DeLand, Florida. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. We have written it to be clear and plain — if anything is unclear, please contact us using the details below.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to give us. Specifically, when you complete our interest form, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your academic major
  • Your expected graduation year
  • Your written response about why you want to join

We do not use cookies, trackers, advertising pixels, or analytics tools on this website. We do not automatically collect your location, browsing history, or device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Review and evaluate your interest in joining Hatter Consulting
  • Contact you about membership, interviews, meetings, and club activities
  • Maintain internal records of prospective and active members

We will not use your information for any purpose unrelated to the operation of the club without your consent.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. Your information is accessible only to Hatter Consulting's executive board members who need it to carry out club operations. We may share information with Stetson University administration or our faculty advisor where reasonably necessary for the club's functioning or where required by university policy.

Our interest form is processed through FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party form-delivery service that transmits your submission to our email on our behalf. FormSubmit handles your data in accordance with its own privacy terms. We use it solely to receive form submissions and for no other purpose.

How We Store and Protect Your Information

We retain your information only for as long as it is relevant to your involvement with the club. We take reasonable measures to keep it secure and limit access to authorized officers. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You may, at any time, request that we:

  • Tell you what information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information from our records

To make any of these requests, simply email us at the address below and we will respond promptly.

Students Under 18

This website is intended for current and prospective undergraduate students. If you are under 18, please submit your information only with the awareness and permission of a parent or guardian.

External Links

Our website may link to external sites, such as LinkedIn. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites, and we encourage you to review their policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
